How It Works

The MARION system uses a network of advanced sensors to detect potential intrusions. When a threat is detected, the system activates our unique dog bark alarm, creating the impression of a guard dog on the premises.

  1. Intrusion Detection: Our sensors identify any unauthorized entry attempts.
  2. Bark Activation: The system triggers realistic dog barking sounds.
  3. Alert System: You receive instant notifications on your preferred devices.
  4. Deterrence: Intruders are scared off by the convincing sound of a guard dog.
